# Ecosystem Impact

## Ecosystem impact

StarkFlow is designed to make Starknet easier to use and more active.

It focuses on practical flows that users can complete end to end.

### Impact areas

* Onboard non-technical users with gasless flows.
* Reduce friction in DeFi interaction.
* Increase transaction volume through automation.
* Expand Starknet reach across chains while keeping Starknet central.

### Alignment with Starknet

StarkFlow demonstrates real use of account abstraction.

It shows how paymasters improve onboarding and retention.

It also creates more repeatable onchain activity through execution and automation.
