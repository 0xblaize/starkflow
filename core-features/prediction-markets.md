# Prediction Markets

## Prediction markets

StarkFlow adds onchain prediction markets for hedging and ecosystem participation.

The design connects market activity with the broader execution layer.

### Current scope

* Hedge positions with market exposure.
* Settle outcomes with oracle data.
* Keep prediction flows close to user execution.

### Upcoming expansion

Upcoming mainnet work expands this into a higher-activity market layer.

#### Planned capabilities

* Ecosystem activity markets.
* User-generated markets.
* Prediction-driven execution and hedging.

### Settlement

Pragma oracles provide market data for settlement.
