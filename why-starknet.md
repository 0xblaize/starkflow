# Why Starknet

## Why Starknet

StarkFlow is built to use Starknet features directly.

This keeps the user experience simple without hiding core blockchain guarantees.

### Why it fits

* **Account abstraction** enables wallet flows that feel native.
* **Paymasters** make gas sponsorship possible at scale.
* **High throughput** supports automated and repeated execution.

### What StarkFlow adds

StarkFlow turns infrastructure primitives into user-facing workflows.

That includes swaps, bridging, automation, and prediction-driven actions.
