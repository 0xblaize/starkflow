# Problem Statement

## Problem statement

DeFi still asks users to manage gas, bridges, wallets, and timing.

Each step adds friction before users reach value.

### Main pain points

* Gas costs punish trial and error.
* Cross-chain execution feels fragmented and risky.
* Wallet and transaction management stays too complex.
* Automation like DCA and hedging stays out of reach for most users.

### What this causes

Users drop before they complete a useful workflow.

Protocols lose activation, retention, and transaction volume.
